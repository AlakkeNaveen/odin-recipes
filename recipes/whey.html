Whey Protein Shake Recipe

Description

Indulge in a nutritious and delicious whey protein shake, perfect for fueling your body after a workout or as a quick and satisfying meal replacement. This protein-packed shake combines the smooth creaminess of ripe banana with the wholesome goodness of whey protein powder, blended together with your choice of milk and a handful of almonds for added richness and texture. Whether you're looking to boost your protein intake or satisfy your hunger with a nutritious snack, this whey protein shake is sure to leave you feeling energized and satisfied.

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop whey protein powder
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • Handful of almonds
  • 2 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • 1 teaspoon of chia seeds

Steps

  1. In a blender, combine whey protein powder, banana slices, milk, almonds, peanut butter, and chia seeds.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy your delicious whey protein shake!
